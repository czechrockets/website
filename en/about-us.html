About us

We got this. We want to get Czech Republic to space (even more)… or to get closer at least.

Czech Rocket Society is made for students that are interested in rocketry and cosmonautics. We want to give them an opportunity to use the knowledge they got at school, but mainly to experiment, try and fail, build with their own hands and only more forward. Unlike other teams or projects, we want to have a comprehensive approach to rockets, be always professional and dutiful. However, we are not only searching for rocket engineers. As we are a self-sustaining society, graphic designers, lawyers, videomakers and others are very welcome too!

The Society works as a little parent company for all its projects. It is not only rocketry, but also all the management, procedures and organization around it. As for our vision, we would like to build similar society to those best in Europe and in the world. In the future we are planning to compete on European rocketry challenges, build even more complicated rockets and maybe even reach space one day. For many students, this could be a great opportunity to connect their studies with their passion.
