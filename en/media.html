Where you could have seen us...

We are passionate about astronautics and rockets and like to participate in various events related to these fields. As part of our activities, the Czech Rocket Society also educates young enthusiasts about astronautics. Here are some of the events where we have represented Czech enthusiasm and passion for rocket science.

Náměšťské dny kosmonautiky 2023

Náměšť nad Oslavou | 21.-24. BŘEZNA 2022

Ve dnech 21.-24. března se konaly Náměšťské dny kosmonautiky, kterých jsme se účastnili. Akce byla velmi povedené a rádi dorazímě příští rok znovu. Těšilo nás i setkání se slovenským kosmonautem Ivanem Bellou a záložním astronautem ESA kpt. Ing Alešem Svobodou. Děkujeme pořadatelům za pozvání.

MAKER FAIRE BRNO 2022

Výstaviště Brno | 22. - 23. říjen 2022

Na první brněnský Maker Faire, kterého se spolek Czech Rocket Society účastnil, jsme přivezli ukázat naši raketu Sherpa z národního finále CanSat včetně kompletní rampy. Stali jsme se tak velmi výrazným vystavovatelem, který byl dominantou haly brněnského výstaviště.

Veletrh UniTrh (BEST Prague)

FIT ČVUT, PRAHA 13. DUBNA 2022

As part of the University IT competition, we were part of the accompanying program UniTrh. The whole event was organized by students from BEST Prague, which is part of the Student Union of CTU. At our booth, we presented our previous rockets and showed the parachute from our CanSat Launcher.,

Prague Technika magazine 02/22

DIGITÁLNÍ PODOBA ZDE | únor 2022

Czech Rocket Society's activities were also featured in a two-page spread in the February issue of the Prague Technika newsletter published by the CTU Publishing House. The newsletter is distributed to university workplaces, ministries, and selected companies. You can read the digital version of the two-page spread and the rest of the issue here.

Czech Republic Calling to Space(ESERO)

NEXT ZONE SMÍCHOV, PRAHA | 24. LISTOPADU 2021

Czech Rocket Society collaborated with ESERO (European Space Education Resource Office) on the program for the event "Czech Republic Calling to Space", where we organized a workshop titled "How to Build a Rocket". The main part of the program was a live conversation with Matthiasem Maurer , an astronaut of the European Space Agency who was at the International Space Station at the time. You can watch the recording of the conversation at this link.

ŽIVÝ PŘENOS NA SLIDESLIVE | 12. LISTOPADU 2021

During the Czech Space Week, the largest space activities festival in the Czech Republic, two members of the Czech Rocket Society hosted a live broadcast called "Students’ Rocket Stopař or How to Build a Rocket", aimed mainly at younger viewers. You can watch the recording here, with the beginning of the performance starting at 2:26:32.

Innovation week 2021

Planetárium Praha | 15. ŘÍJNA 2021

Our rocket Stopař - already flown several times by then - was comprehensively presented by representatives of individual departments of this project at the Innovation Week at the Prague Planetarium in Holešovice.

Representatives from our project, Stopař, presented our rocket comprehensively at the Innovation Week at the Prague Planetarium in Holešovice. By that time, Stopař had already flown several times.

CZECH ROCKET CHALLENGE '21

LETIŠTĚ BRNO-MEDLÁNKY | 25. ZÁŘÍ 2021

We helped to prepare the first year of theCzech Rocket Challenge. student competition. On this occasion, we launched our historically first rocket, Stopař. Although we did not participate in the competition, we appeared on TV (here at 3:18:30). However, two members of the society successfully participated in the competition as part of the Xperience Aerospace team - Jan Němec and Kryštof Hájek, who set the bar for the next years with their Trinidad and Tobago rocket reaching an incredible 971 meters and winning the first prize. In 2022, the Czech Rocket Society will become the organizer of this competition, in cooperation with other partners.

Výstaviště holešovice, Praha | 11.-12. ZÁŘÍ 2021

We attended the fourth annual festival of modern craftsmen, Maker Faire Prague 2021, and were among other 500 exhibitors. More than 6,500 visitors visited the festival. The society prepared not only a stand with real parts of their rocket Stopař, but also a presentation on the main stage and a workshop describing rocket construction.
