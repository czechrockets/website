Hitchhiker

A launcher, able to ascend up to 500 metres, it has been mainly meant to inspire public with its unique payload and to start the CRS activities. That is HitchHiker. It will stay the first CRS project forever.

LEARN MORE

CanSat Launcher

A launcher built to support high-school competition CanSat is the current flagship project of Czech Rocket Society. The payload consists of minisatellites made by high-school students. Those are taken by our launcher to 1 kilometer, where the minisatellites are deployed.

LEARN MORE

Solids

Solids Team is researching and developing solid fuel powered rocket motors. They are also working on a new motor Ikarus for the CanSat launcher (Czech Rocket Society current flagship project).

Control

Control Team is researching and developing means of active and passive rockets control systems (attitude control). It will be very important team for the future Czech Rocket Society projects, but also for other teams.
