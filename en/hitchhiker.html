Hitchhiker

THE FIRST STEP...

Project Hitchhiker is the first ever project of the Czech Rocket Society, which got its name from a very famous fantasy novel The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams. The rocket is very similar to the ones built for Czech Rocket Challenge technically, however, Hitchhiker's components are all built by the members of CRS themselves.

Hitchhiker is a rocket of approximately one meter, and is worked on by three departments;

Propulsion

working on the rocket engine, which is going to be powered by solid propellants.

Avionics

solving electronics for data collection and connection to the ground station.

Structures

The body will be made of composite and will have a detachable tip, in which there will be a parachute for a safe landing.

Hitchhiker will also have a selfie stick on board, through which it will pull out its parachute and also capture a surprise that is yet to be revealed.

So far we are done with the theoretical part of the project including documentation (for people interested, please do not hesitate to contact us via email) and the departments are starting to develop and build their components. We were able to get feedback from professors and faculty directors of the Czech Technical University and hence move forward in the project thanks to CDR.

We will inform you about our future steps so please do not hesitate and follow us on social media.

Hitchhikers take-off is planned for autumn 2021 during the Czech Rocket Challenge.
