Planetum is a company that includes Plantarium Prague, Stefanik Observatory, and Dablice Observatory. The word "planetarium" refers to a projector designed to project stars, the Sun, the Moon, planets, and other celestial objects. And the Planetarium Prague has the LARGEST projection surface in the Czech Republic. The projection can be viewed thanks to a unique technology of spatial representation of the universe in all weather conditions and in 8K resolution.

Stefanik Observatory, located at the top of Petrin Hill, has been offering unique night sky observation with its large telescopes for almost 100 years.

Dablice Observatory is a great place for beginner astronomers with daytime and nighttime sky observation. A rich program and a permanent astronomy exhibition are prepared for school excursions.

At the Planetarium Prague, you will feel like you are in the real universe.

STRATOSYST is a Czech company that develops HAPS (High Altitude Pseudo-Satellites), which are vehicles that stay in the air for an extended period of time at an altitude of several tens of kilometers. These vehicles are often atmospheric balloons or airplanes and are used for Earth observation and imaging, as well as for telecommunications and navigation, defense, astronomy, and testing new technologies.

For these purposes, Stratosyst has developed the multifunctional Skyrider balloon, which can carry up to 12 kg of payload and operate for several months. Skyrider is reusable and emission-free.

Stratosyst is also proud to have won the international Gallileo Masters 2018 award and to have been included in the top 50 ESA startup
